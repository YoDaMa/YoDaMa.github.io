About Maguire and Co.

Maguire and Co. is just me, and my thoughts. It's like my personal branding, except not legitimate at all and I just really like how it sounds.

I am a junior at Rice University studying Electrical and Computer Engineering, specializing in Data Science. Electricity has intrigued me since I was young, and I chose my major because it was something I wanted to learn. My intellectual journey through the field has been difficult but also incredible, and the more I learn the more captivated I become.

I was born in Bangkok, Thailand. At age three I moved to Balikpapan, Indonesia; a small city on the southeast coast of the island of Borneo. At age 7 I moved to Jakarta, Indoensia, and at age 10 I moved to Cairo, Egypt, the city where my mother grew up and where many of my relatives on her side of the family live. At age 13, entering 8th grade, I moved again, this time to Houston, Texas, where I have remained ever since.

I enjoy good coffee, most any art form from the impressionist era, history, and probability. My ideal spot is reading, writing, or thinking in a quiet place. I'm introverted, but challenge myself by practicing public speaking, and performing as a player in Rice University's Spontaneous Combustion Improv Group. I consider it one of the most intellectual forms of comedy, but really it's just ridiculous.
