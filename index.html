About

YoDaMa stands for Yoseph Daniel Maguire, taking the first two letters of my first middle and last name and combining them. I quite like how it sounds, too.

I am a junior at Rice University studying Electrical and Computer Engineering, specializing in Data Science. Electricity has intrigued me since I was young, and I chose my major because it was something I wanted to learn. My intellectual journey through the field has been difficult, but also incredible, and the more I learn the more captivated by the knowledge I become.

I was born in Bangkok, Thailand. At age three I moved to Balikpapan, Indonesia, a small city on the southeast coast of the island of Borneo. By 2nd Grade I moved to Jakarta, Indoensia, and entering 5th Grade I moved to Cairo, Egypt, the capital of Egypt and the city where my mother grew up. At age 13, entering 8th grade, I moved again, this time to Houston, Texas, where I have remained ever since.

I enjoy good coffee, most any art form from the impressionist era, history, and probability. My ideal spot is reading, writing, or thinking in a quiet place. I'm introverted, but challenge myself by practicing public speaking, and performing as a player in Rice University's Spontaneous Combustion Improv Group.
